Detailed Description

PreferenceGP A Gaussian Process implementation that handles ordered pairs of preferences for the training data rather than direct absolute samples.

Essentially optimizes the solution of the samples given to the GP.

Based off of the math given in: Pairwise Judgements and Absolute Ratings with Gaussian Process Priors

  • a collection of technical details (2014) Bjorn Sand Jenson, Jens Brehm, Nielsen
