AbsBoundProbit This is almost directly Nick's code, for absolute bounded inputs.

Pairwise Judgements and Absolute Ratings with Gaussian Process Priors

  • a collection of technical details (2014) Bjorn Sand Jenson, Jens Brehm, Nielsen
